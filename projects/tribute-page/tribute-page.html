Hello Bauman!

Essentials facts about Bauman's life:

  • 1925 Born in Poznań, Poland
  • 1939 The war begin and Bauman and his family escape into the URSS
  • 1945 Becomes a police officer in the Internal Security Corps, a military unit formed to combat Ukrainian nationalist insurgents and part of the remnants of the Polish Home Army, and starts to study sociology at the Warsaw Academy of Political and Social Science
  • 1953 His father has approach the Israeli embassy in Warsaw with a view to emigrating to Israel, and Bauman is suddenly discharged from the military work
  • 1954 Unemployed, he completes an MA in sociology and the next year became a lecturer at the University of Warsaw
  • 1959 His most important book, titled British socialist movement, has been published
  • 1968 Because of a political purge Bauman, along with other Communist Poles of Jewish descent, leave the country and moves to Israel, teaching sociology at the University of Tel-Aviv and, shortly later, at the University of Leeds in England
  • 1990 His reputation is grewing so fast to exerts a considerable influence on the anti- or alter-globalization movement
  • 2017 Dies at the age of 91
